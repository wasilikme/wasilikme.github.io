The Bluesmen of Newport News!

Who we are

Young, wild, and loud! We are a completely student-led blues group at Christopher Newport University in Newport News, VA.

What we play

The Midnight Bluesmen are bringing classic blues sounds to a modern era! We have a great selection of both original songs and covers of our favorites!

How to book us

We're always looking for places to perform! You can contact us at midnightbluesmen@gmail.com
